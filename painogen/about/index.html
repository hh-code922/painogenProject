About Us

OUR STORY

Summer of 2016, our founder of the company, had a nagging pain due to a pinched nerve, from a regular jogging routine. Having tried the conventional pain killers before that had little to no effect, he started researching on natural solutions to his problem. After all conventional pain killers both oral and topical were not only ineffective, but also gave undesirable side effects ranging from heartburn, jitters to stinging, dry skin.

Frustration led him to seek a clean, side effect free remedy for his problem. He turned to his long time friend and a holistic naturopathy doctor First and Last name who introduced him to a treasure trove of natural remedies. Necessity is truly the mother of all inventions – his quest for a natural pain remedy led to the formulation of Painogen Natural Pain Relief oil. Painogen Natural Pain Relief oil is the result of his personal experience, knowledge of a trained naturopathic doctor along with a team of researchers.

OUR VISION

Mother Nature has a solution for almost every health issue. We want to research these solutions and offer a holistic and natural alternative with proven results. We strive to improve the quality of people’s live through our natural health solutions. Our goal is to change the way people approach pain relief solutions. We want to offer a clean, natural, organic and high quality products for pain relief and other health issues.

OUR VISION

Life is beautiful; minor conditions like aches and pains should not occupy your precious time. Our mission is to help restore your health through well-researched, scientifically-backed natural remedies and solutions. We do the research, so you don’t have to spend time on natural solutions. We develop natural remedies from organic and natural ingredients only.

OUR SOURCING & QUALITY

We have a rigorous process of selecting our suppliers and our stringent quality testing ensures every product we sell is 100% organic with natural ingredients. We directly source our ingredients from the most trusted and thoroughly vetted suppliers. We ensure that our suppliers meet our stringent standards before sourcing from them. Conventional pain killers give symptomatic pain relief but Painogen Natural Pain Reliever addresses the root causes of pain, inflammation and poor blood circulation.
